About

This website aims to display product results from multiple e-commerce sites to help facilitate their comparison and help users make an informed choice.

USAGE GUIDE
Our crawler is still quite rudimentary and hence, it is best to provide search queries as specific as possible and use our filters to further narrow down the results to your liking.
The miscellaneous is very helpful if you used properly. It can help find brands, model numbers etc.
NOTE: Search query and filter specifications must be submitted at once.
TECHNICAL DETAILS
The e-commerce websites that have been scraped till date are:
  • AJIO
  • AMAZON
  • EBAY
Due to some technical details, results are weighted towards AMAZON and EBAY.
NOTE: All product prices are in Indian Rupees(INR). EBAY displays prices in dollars, so it is converted to rupees by multiplying the value with a factor of 72.81 to deliver an approximation rounded to 2 places. EBAY also provides a range for prices, so their average is displayed.
Any feedback/suggestions are welcome.
